Directions

Please see the map above or use the map link here. Directions from the Burbank Airport and LAX is shown below.

From Burbank Airport

  • Follow signs to I-5 South
  • Take I-5 to 134 East
  • 134 East becomes 210 East, into Pasadena
  • Exit I-210 at Lake Street
  • Take Lake South to Del Mar Blvd
  • Take Del Mar East (3 blocks) to Wilson Avenue
  • Take Wilson South (1.5 blocks) to Beckman Institute

From LAX

  • Follow signs to I-105 East
  • Take I-105 to I-110 North
  • Take I-110 North into Pasadena
  • this turns into Arroyo Parkway
  • Take Arroyo North to California Blvd
  • Take California East to Wilson Avenue
  • Take Wilson North (1.5 blocks) to Beckman Institute
